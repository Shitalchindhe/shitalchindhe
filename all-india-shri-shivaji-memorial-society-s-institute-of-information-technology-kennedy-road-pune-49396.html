AISSMS IOIT Courses and Fees 2021

The Institute offers BE, ME and PhD programmes. Some of the fast facts related to AISSMS IOIT courses are mentioned below:

  • AISSMS IOIT offers BTech in six specialisations. The duration of this programme is four years and seat intake is 540.
  • AISSMS IOIT offers ME in two specialisations. The duration of this course is two years and the seat intake is 36.
  • The Institute offers PhD in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. In total, eight candidates are allotted per guide.

AISSMS IOIT Admission 2021

AISSMS IOIT admissions are based on merit. Interested candidates can apply for the course of their choice in offline mode. Admission to BE programme is based on JEE Main or MHT CET score. AISSMS IOIT ME admission is based on marks scored in GATE. AISSMS IOIT Pune admission details are mentioned below:

AISSMS IOIT Scholarships 2021

AISSMS Pune provides various scholarships to students. The Institute awards scholarships to students belonging to the economically weak class, reserved category students and girl students. Some of the AISSMS scholarships are mentioned below:

  • Rajarshri Chattrapati Shahu Maharaj scholarship (Economically Backward Class): This scholarship is offered to only Maharashtra state students belonging to the open category. Under this scholarship scheme, 50 per cent of tuition fee and exam fee is waived off. To avail this scholarship, the income of parents should be less than Income less than Rs 8 LPA.
  • Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh hostel allowance scholarship: The scholarship is awarded to students belonging to Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur and Aurangabad regions. AISSMS IOIT scholarship amount is Rs 3000 per month (scholarship for a total of 10 months).
